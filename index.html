Privacy Policy for Qrnotes

Developer: Cognito Innovations
Effective Date: 16 January 2026

Looking to delete your account? Please see Account Deletion Request below for steps and what data is deleted/retained.

Cognito Innovations (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the Qrnotes mobile application (“App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

  • Name and Email Address: Collected when you sign in using Google Sign-In.

Camera Permission

Qrnotes requests access to your device’s camera only to scan QR codes within the app.

We do not take, store, or upload photos/videos from your camera.

How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account in the App
  • Provide core app functionality (login and user identification)
  • Respond to support requests

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information.

We do not share your personal information with third parties except Google Sign-In for authentication as part of the login process.

Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Account Deletion Request

This section applies to accounts created in Qrnotes by Cognito Innovations (as shown on the Google Play Store listing).

How to request account deletion

  1. Email us at botcognito@gmail.com with the subject: Qrnotes Account Deletion Request
  2. In the email, include the Google account email you used to sign in to Qrnotes.
  3. We will process the deletion request within 7 days.

Data that will be deleted

  • Your account information associated with Qrnotes (such as Name and Email collected via Google Sign-In).
  • Your account profile data (Name, Email) and any Qrnotes records linked to your account stored in Firestore.

Data that may be retained

  • We do not maintain separate backup copies of your personal data.
  • After your deletion request is processed, your account-related data stored in our database (Firestore) is deleted.
  • We may retain limited information only if required to comply with legal obligations or to resolve disputes.

Children’s Privacy

Qrnotes is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

You can stop using the App at any time by uninstalling it. If you want your account-related data removed, contact us using the steps above.
